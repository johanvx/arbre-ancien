# I Would like to Start Another Blog

It has been over 3 years since I last wrote a blog post. It's time to start over.

I've been looking for a good framework for my blog site, but I never found one.
That was a huge excuse for me not writing: I wouldn't have any motivation until
I built a beautiful blog site.

That was true, and most of the time, I just didn't would like to do anything because
I didn't feel enough of an aesthetic.

These days, I occasionally read other people's posts to inspire me to build my
own blog site. Fortunately, besides blog design inspiration, my mindset toward
taking action has also been refreshed.

Summarizing all the posts that inspired me would probably take a lot of time,
and I don't plan on doing that, so I'd rather just put a reading list here:

- [90% of everything is crap] by Critter (Mike Crittenden)
- [Consistency before quality] by Critter
- [How to come up with blog post ideas] by Robert Heaton
- [Why I Write] by Cheng-Wei Hu

[90% of everything is crap]: https://critter.blog/2021/01/20/90-of-everything-is-crap/
[Consistency before quality]: https://critter.blog/2021/01/18/consistency-beats-quality/
[How to come up with blog post ideas]: https://robertheaton.com/2019/09/24/how-to-come-up-with-blog-post-ideas/
[Why I Write]: https://chengweihu.com/writing-manifesto/
